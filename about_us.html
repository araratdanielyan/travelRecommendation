ABOUT US

Travel Pal has grown from a local agency on the Southern California coast to a nationwide network of top travel agents serving
travelers all over the world. We’ve spent half a century building relationships with the best global travel partners. These relationships
are our secret — they’re what allow our expert agents to create unique, memorable itineraries for clients with deals and perks they won’t find
anywhere else. Travel Pal agents handle every facet of travel planning for every kind of traveler — cruisers, explorers, beachcombers, experiential
travelers, corporate travelers, and everyone in between.
